Intelligent Forward Collision Warning

Save your bumper. Altima with Rear Automatic Braking watches out directly behind you for large stationary objects you might not see. If it detects something and you fail to stop, it can automatically engage the brakes to help avoid a rear collision or lessen the severity of an impact.

Blind Spot Warning

The 2024 Nissan Altima features a sophisticated blind-spot warning system, enhancing safety on the road. Using advanced sensors and cameras, it alerts drivers to vehicles in their blind spots, reducing the risk of accidents during lane changes. This technology provides peace of mind by helping drivers make more informed decisions and preventing potential collisions.

Rear Cross Traffic Alert

Fear no parking lot. When you’re backing out of a space, Rear Cross Traffic Alert watches around the rear of your Altima and can warn you about cars it detects creeping up on you from either side.
